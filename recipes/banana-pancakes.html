Description

Crowd-pleasing banana pancakes made from scratch that are ready in minutes. A fun twist on ordinary pancakes.

Wake up on the right side of the bed with a stack of sweet, cozy, and simple banana pancakes. This top-rated banana pancake recipe is easy to make and it comes together in just 15 minutes, so you don't have to wake up early to enjoy a satisfying breakfast. Learn how to make, store, and serve the best banana pancakes ever.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • An Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine your dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder) in one bowl and your wet ingredients (egg, milk, vegetable oil, mashed bananas) in another bowl.
  2. Pour the batter in Â¼ cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat.
  3. Serve your banana pancakes immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite pancake toppings.
